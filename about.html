My Design Philosophy

When it comes to programming in games, I often seek to create quick and simple tools to make for my designers. In many of my projects, especially more recent ones, you'll find a lot of drag and drop scripts for testing mechanics. Keeping things compartmentalized and organized is a big focus for me in any project I work on.
