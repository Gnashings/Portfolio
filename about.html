My name is Nash!

Having grown up with two Caribbean parents, you can usually find me blasting reggae music while cooking up a storm. Catch me in a discord call most nights hanging out with friends, nerding out over paleontology books, or wondering around by a lake trying to scout a good spot to throw some lines!

When it comes to programming games, my general philosophy is that you should make everyones lives easier. Keeping things compartmentalized and organized is a big focus for me, or ensuring that things are easily tweakable/testible by designers and the team. While I've previously worked on systems and gameplay, I've been heavily leaning into more technical art recently. It just seems to be a marriage of two things I absolutely love.
